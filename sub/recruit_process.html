01

Application Submission

Anyone who meets the qualifications for the position is welcome to apply, regardless of age, gender, or nationality.
Please submit an application form with basic information such as personal details, educational background, and a self-introduction.
Brownskin respects the individuality and potential of each applicant. In addition to the basic form, you are welcome to submit additional materials in any format to showcase your unique qualities.

02

Document Review

We carefully review whether applicants meet the job performance capabilities and qualifications specified in the job posting.
After the application period ends, experts in the relevant field will review the documents and comprehensively evaluate the cover letter and work experience. This process helps us determine how well the applicant's values align with Brownskin's talent philosophy.
The results of the document review will be available on the recruitment page, and it will take approximately 1-2 weeks for notification.

03

Interview Screening

The interview consists of a first-round practical interview and a second-round language and executive interview.
Interview schedules are individually notified to those who pass the document screening, and schedules are adjusted to accommodate the applicant's convenience.
It takes approximately 1-2 weeks from the interview date to notify the results.

04

Final Selection

Those who pass all stages will be individually notified of compensation negotiations and onboarding procedures.

05

Hiring Completion

Once compensation and the start date are finalized, you will join Brownskin as a new team member.
We sincerely welcome and congratulate you on joining Brownskin after this lengthy process.
