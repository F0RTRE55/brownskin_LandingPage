About Brownskin

Brownskin is a social enterprise creating eco-friendly regenerated leather from agricultural byproducts.
We transform discarded resources into valuable materials, promoting a sustainable future and shared growth with local communities.

Welcome to Brownskin.

We are a social enterprise dedicated to transforming agricultural and food by-products into high-performance regenerated leather. Our mission is to prove that sustainability and functionality can coexist — that waste can be reborn as beautiful, durable materials that serve both people and the planet.

Since our founding in 2022, we have worked closely with local farmers, material scientists, and designers to create leather alternatives that meet the highest standards in quality, aesthetics, and environmental responsibility. Every square meter we produce not only diverts waste from landfills but also creates local jobs and supports circular economy practices.

We invite partners, brands, and creators to join us in shaping a more sustainable future for materials.
